  • Kitchen cleanup

Temple Maintenance

Help maintain the cleanliness and beauty of our sacred temple premises.

1-2 hours Flexible
  • Cleaning temple areas
  • Gardening and landscaping
  • Decoration for festivals

3-4 hours Event-based

  • Guest coordination
  • Decoration and arrangements

Education Support

2-3 hours Weekly
  • Teaching assistance
  • Material preparation
  • Student support

Community Outreach

Participate in community service activities and outreach programs.

2-4 hours Monthly
  • Medical camps
  • Educational workshops
  • Social welfare activities

Aarti & Puja Assistance

Help with daily aarti ceremonies and special puja arrangements.

1-2 hours Daily/Weekly
  • Aarti preparation
  • Puja material arrangement
  • Ceremony assistance
