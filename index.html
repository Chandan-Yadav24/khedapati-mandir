About Our Mandir

मंदिर के बारे में

Khedapati Sarkar Mandir is a sacred sanctuary dedicated to divine worship and community service. Nestled in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, our temple has been a beacon of spiritual light for devotees seeking peace, blessings, and connection with the divine.

Founded with the mission to spread love, compassion, and spiritual wisdom, we welcome all devotees to experience the divine presence and participate in our sacred rituals and community services.
